Seattle Podcasters Guild

We are a group of Seattle area podcasters. The group has a dual mission. First, we want to support and learn from each other as podcasters. Second, we want to help further popularize podcast listening in the area.

Meetings

Our next meetup will be at Optimism Brewing Company on Tuesday February 20 at 6PM. We will be on the east side of the building. Look for a red ballon. This time for real!

Keep up with future meeting by revisiting this site or joining our Meetup Group.

Everyone is welcome! Doesn't matter if you are a podcasting veteran or someone that is just thinking of starting a show. You just need to want to talk about podcasting. Come on down and join us!
